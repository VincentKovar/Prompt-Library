## YOUR TASK

Rewrite the source content to match the target voice profile by:

1. ANALYZE THE TARGET VOICE:
   - Study tone descriptors, vocabulary patterns, sentence structure
   - Note "always do" and "never do" rules
   - Understand personality markers and how they express themselves

2. REWRITE THE CONTENT to match:
   - Replace vocabulary: use their signature words, avoid their forbidden words
   - Match sentence rhythm: short if they write short, long if they write long, mixed if they mix
   - Adopt their tone: if they're direct, be direct; if they're witty, add wit
   - Follow their formatting: their emoji use, line breaks, capitalization, paragraph structure
   - Mirror personality: their humor style, vulnerability level, confidence level
   - Use their phrases: if they say "the truth is," use it; if they never use "honestly," don't
   - Match specificity: if they give numbers, give numbers; if they're vague, stay vague

3. VERIFY THE MATCH:
   - Does this sound like them?
   - Would they publish this as-is?
   - Does it pass the voice test from their profile?
